FAQ

Who We Are

We are a team of passionate individuals who care about helping the average consumer improve or maintain their financial health, save money, build credit and make it all easy!

What We Do

Our products are designed to help you make transactions easily and with confidence that you are within your budget and maximizing potential perks and rewards. After all, a penny saved is a penny earned. :)

Why should I use Sense instead of other personal finance apps?

At Sense, we focus on taking the thought out of healthy financial habits, proactively keeping you within your budget, optimizing your perks and rewards by identifying the best card to use for any given purchase, and tons of other helpful features!

What are some of the benefits you help me get?

We want to make it easy as as possible for you to take advantage of all the card benefits you are offered, including extended warranty, zero fraud liability, car rental insurance, exclusive concert tickets, hotel or flight upgrades and much more!

How do you maximize my savings?

Our algorithm processes the different rewards available to you based on the variety of benefits your different credit and debit cards offer you. When you tell us where you are making a purchase, we take your preferences into account and then recommend the best card to give you the most savings and other benefits possible, all while keeping your credit score healthy.

What other features do your produts have?

We take our mission to make financial health for everyone as attainable as possible. For this reason, we offer as many tools for you to create and keep a budget and increase your credit score as possible! We are adding new products all the time and would love to hear it if you have a suggestion.

Which platforms are your products available on?

Currently our apps are available on ios, android, and the chrome browser. Want added support elsewhere? Let us know!

How much does it cost?

Our app is free to use and download, with some optional features available for a fee.

What is your data sharing policy?

We take privacy seriously. Sense will never share your personal data without your permission. For more information, read our privacy policy and terms of service.
